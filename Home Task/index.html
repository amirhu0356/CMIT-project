OUR INTRODUCTION

Mission

To ensure efficient and corruption free working in all Government Departments including Autonomous / Semi-Autonomous Bodies and to help the people in redressal of their grievances.

✔ Governance Oversight

Monitoring departments to ensure transparency, efficiency, and adherence to government policies.

✔ Public Interest

Taking cognizance of issues affecting citizens and recommending corrective and preventive measures.
